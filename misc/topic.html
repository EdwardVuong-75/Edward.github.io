Robot for Restaurants and Fear of Unemployment

The implementation of AI technology in most modern day jobs is still quite rare. The widespread use of robots for machine automation have been recognized for jobs that demand precision and speed: in the cases of machine assembly lines and factories. The use of AI and robots in Hospitality are being experimented on by some Hotels and Food service establishments, and is an ongoing development that has recently gained traction on social media. Linking back to the fear of unemployment for wait staff as well as kitchen staff.

ex. Martin, C. D., Huff, C., Gotterbarn, D., & Miller, K. (1996). A framework for implementing and teaching the social and ethical impact of computing. Education and Information Technologies, 1(2), 101-122.
